What we learned
Keyword selection matters. "Virtual summit" and "masterclass" returned almost no overlap with "webinar." Run all variations or you miss event types entirely.
Profile lookup has a 20% false positive rate. Name + company fuzzy matching is fast but imprecise. Apify validation at $0.01/profile catches mismatches before they hit a sequence.
Validation is first-name-only — not current company. The lookup sometimes returns a correct profile where the listed company is a previous role. Rejecting on company mismatch would be wrong.
Apify dev_fusion actor: 35 attempted, 35 returned. No failures. Most reliable $0.01 identity check available for LinkedIn.
