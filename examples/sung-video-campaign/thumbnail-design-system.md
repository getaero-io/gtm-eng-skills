# Thumbnail Design System

These thumbnails follow the Deepline marketing-site design language from `/Users/jaitoor/dev/deepline-api/src/app/deepline-site.css` and the live homepage at `deepline.com`.

## Brand Tokens

- Paper: `#fafaf8`
- Paper 2: `#f2eee4`
- Foreground plum: `#1c112a`
- Deep plum: `#2a1b3d`
- Electric indigo: `#1919ff`
- Soft indigo: `#a5b4fc`
- Border: `#d7d7e2`

## Type

- Display: Deepline local Geist heavy as the closest local stand-in for the site's Switzer display face.
- Mono: Deepline local Geist Mono for eyebrows and terminal snippets.
- Body: Deepline local Geist medium.

## Layout Schema

- 1280 x 720 canvas.
- Cream paper background with subtle plum mini-grid and right-edge dither.
- Real Deepline wordmark at top left.
- Mono eyebrow with leading indigo hairline.
- Large plum headline on the left.
- Product screenshot/video frame on the right, framed like a site surface.
- Terminal command block below the headline.
- `deepline.com` as the only website reference.

## Anti-Patterns

- No generic dark overlay cards.
- No teal-only palette.
- No stock SaaS glow background.
- No old Deepline domain references.
